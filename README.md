# 🌿Sobral & Monteiro Consulting

Environmental consulting — ecology, field monitoring, and impact studies across Portugal and Africa.

We cover the full project lifecycle: from environmental impact studies and licensing support to long-term field monitoring and reporting. Our team works across a wide range of sectors including wind farms, solar parks, roads, railways, water dams, ports, airports, and more.

**Services include:**
- Avifauna and bat monitoring
- Flora, vegetation and fauna surveys
- Environmental impact studies
- Project monitoring and follow-up
- Spatial data via our WebSIG platform

📍 Portugal · Angola · Mozambique · Cabo Verde · São Tomé and Príncipe
